Frequently Asked Questions

Earning to Give is a strategy where you choose a high-earning career specifically to donate a significant portion to high-impact causes. It's based on the insight that funding effective interventions can create more good than working directly on them.
The most common commitment is the Giving What We Can pledge — 10% of your pre-tax income. Many EA members in high-income careers donate 20–50%. Even 1% from a diaspora salary makes a meaningful difference in the Somali context.
It depends on your skills and circumstances. For many people, especially in finance, tech, law, or medicine, the counterfactual impact of their donation exceeds what they could do directly. Our community discussions can help you think this through.
Software engineering, medicine, finance, law, consulting, and international development tend to offer the highest salaries. Diaspora professionals earning in USD, GBP, or EUR are particularly well-positioned to maximize this strategy.
