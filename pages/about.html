🌱 New Organisation · Feb 2026

We exist to help Somalia's talent
go where it's needed most.

EA Somalia launched in February 2026 with a small, passionate group of ~50 Somali professionals. We're at the very beginning of what we hope will be a meaningful journey.

Our Mission

Help Somali professionals identify and pursue the most impactful careers and giving opportunities.

We use evidence, reason, and a commitment to impartial concern for all people. We believe Somalia has extraordinary potential — a young, connected population, a growing diaspora with international experience, and a set of challenges that are simultaneously urgent and tractable with the right talent deployed at scale.

By connecting talent to the best frameworks, communities, and opportunities, we aim to catalyze a generation of Somali professionals making measurable, evidence-based progress on hard problems.

Our Core Values

🔍

Evidence First

We prioritize interventions with rigorous evidence. When evidence is limited, we apply systematic frameworks and are transparent about uncertainty.

🌍

Impartial Concern

We care about improving wellbeing regardless of nationality, proximity, or identity — equal concern for all people.

💡

Intellectual Honesty

We update our views with new evidence, welcome disagreement, and acknowledge when we're wrong. Dogmatism is incompatible with doing good well.

🤝

Open Community

Everyone can learn and contribute regardless of where they start. EA Somalia is a place to explore and grow together.
