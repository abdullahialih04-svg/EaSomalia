Our Context

Somalia is at a pivotal moment.

With a young, talented population and a growing diaspora, we have an extraordinary opportunity to channel talent toward challenges that matter most.

🍽️

Food Security

Recurring droughts and famine affect millions. Evidence-based agricultural research and policy can save thousands of lives each year.

💻

Digital Infrastructure

Expanding internet access and digital literacy unlocks economic opportunity for millions locked out of the global economy.

💸

Remittance Efficiency

Somalia receives over $1.3B in remittances yearly. Cutting transfer fees even slightly redirects millions toward essential needs.

🌱

Climate-Resilient Farming

Drought-resistant crops and sustainable farming protect livelihoods and food supply as the climate shifts dangerously.
