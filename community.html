Mentorship Program

Get 1-on-1 guidance

Free coaching sessions with experienced professionals who have navigated high-impact career transitions. Available to all EA Somalia community members.

Step 01

Apply

Share your background, career goals, and where you'd benefit from guidance.

Step 02

Get Matched

We match you with a mentor based on career stage, cause areas, and background.

Step 03

4 Sessions

Four 45-minute sessions covering career assessment, strategy, CV review, and planning.

Step 04

Join Community

Access our alumni network, ongoing peer cohort, and job referrals.
